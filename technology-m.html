Same hand, new tricks.

Gest stays snug and secure without getting in the way. The small, light form factor doesn't interfere with how you normally use your hands, allowing you to mix gestures with standard keyboard and mouse actions.

Work all day.

We’ve packed a 500 mAh battery and a Bluetooth Low Energy radio into each controller, giving you eight hours of active use per charge. You can also use Gest from up to 150 feet away from your computer, which comes in handy for presentations.

Fits like a glove.

We've designed Gest to fit your hand, no matter its size. The finger mounts adjust to fit your fingers perfectly, so you can snap them on and off with ease. Nine-axis motion sensors track each finger with sub-millimeter precision.
